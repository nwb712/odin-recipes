Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Easy home-made peanut butter cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup (188g) all purpose flour, scoop and level
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (105g) granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup (110g) packed light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup (185g) creamy peanut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Line two 18 x 13 baking sheets.
  2. In a mixing bowl whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In the bowl of an electric stand mixer, cream together butter with granulated and brown sugar.
  4. Mix in peanut butter, then blend in egg and vanilla. With mixer on low, add in flour mixture slowly just until combined.
  5. Scoop dough and shape into balls (30g or about 1/8 cup). Place on baking sheet two inches apart.
  6. Using a long fork, press perpendicular hatch marks onto the cookies
  7. Bake for 9 minutes one sheet at a time.
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes then transfer onto cooling rack for further cooling.
