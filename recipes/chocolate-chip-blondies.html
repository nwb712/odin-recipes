Chocolate Chip Blondies

Description

A quick and easy recipe for chocolate chip blondies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Combine brown sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla. Mix until blended. Combine with flour, baking powder, and salt. Add in chocolate chips.
  2. Spread in greased 13 x 9 baking tray. Bake at 350F for 18 to 20 minutes.
  3. Let cool and cut into bars
