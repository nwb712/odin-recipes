Flour Tortillas

Description

A simple recipe for making flour tortillas from scratch at home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 lukewarm water

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients. Add butter and water. Mix until combined. If necessary add flour in 1 tbsp increments until combined
  2. Let dough rest 10 minutes. Heat large skillet over medium heat
  3. Divide dough into 12 and roll into 8 inch circles
  4. Cook in skillet about 1 minute each side
